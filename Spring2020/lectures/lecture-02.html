EDS Notebook 3 

Three ways to represent sequences: 

1) tuples: immutable, elements can be different types

2) lists: mutable and resizable, elements can be different types

3) NumPy arrays: mutable but not resizable, all elements the same type (although that type can be object), elementwise functions/operators do what we probably want

Summary statistics:

1) If you have a continuous variable and you have to summarize it with a single number, what would you do?

2) What if you can have two numbers?

Communicating differences:

Suppose you want to convey the idea that sandwiches in Boston are more expensive than sandwiches in London.

1) Absolute difference

2) Relative difference (expressed as a fraction or percentage)

Pros and Cons?

This example demonstrates:

1) With absolute difference, the mean of the difference is the difference of the means.

2) With relative difference, that's not true!

So there are at least three reasonable ways to summarize the sandwich gap.  Which did the Economist choose?
